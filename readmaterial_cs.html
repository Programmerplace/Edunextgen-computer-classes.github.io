History of Computers

From the Abacus to Supercomputers, the journey of computers has transformed human life. Early mechanical devices gave way to electronic computers that now power AI and space research.

Programming Languages

Programming languages like Python, Java, and C++ are the backbone of the digital world. They allow humans to communicate with machines effectively.

Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence (AI) enables computers to mimic human intelligence. From chatbots to self-driving cars, AI is shaping the future of technology.

Networking Basics

Networking connects computers and devices, allowing data sharing across the world. The Internet is the biggest example of a network.
